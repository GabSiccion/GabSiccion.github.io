About us!

We have created this as a partial fulfillment of Capstone. This research study primarily aims to create and develop web-based learning software to have a course program exploration in career planning of Grade 12 Senior High School students of De La Salle University – Dasmariñas which is the Course Explorer. The specific targets are the following: (1) Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math (STEM) Track. (2) Information and Communication Technology (ICT) Track, who will continue to wish to study in the said respective university and undergo to a Computer Science course (either in Information Technology or Computer Science) as one of their career options as they graduate in Senior High.
